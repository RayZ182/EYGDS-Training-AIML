 JBL, founded in 1946 by James B. Lansing, Sr., is a renowned American audio equipment company. Known for their speakers, headphones, and sound systems, JBL's early success came from their development of the D130 cinema speaker. Today, they offer a wide range of products, including home audio systems, car audio equipment, professional audio solutions, and headphones, catering to diverse needs in entertainment, transportation, and professional settings.
